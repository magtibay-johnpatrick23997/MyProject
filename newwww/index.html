How to become a member?

Requirements:

What to fill Out?

Fill out the PMRF

• In order to become a member, first is to fill up the PMRF or the PhilHealth Member Registration Form. This includes the members personal data such as name of the member, maiden name of the member's mother, name of the spouse of the member if they are legally married, birthday of the member, birthplace, sex, civil status, permanent home address, mailing address, and contant number. On the second page, the name of the member in printed form or capitalized with signature above it as well as the date when the documents was signed.

Where do I get the PMRF?

Public Assistance and Complaint’s Desk (PACD) PhilHealth Website (www.philhealth.gov.ph/downloads) or in any PhilHealth Local Insurance Office (LHIO)

• If you have any question or query regarding the PMRF or any other problem or assistance, please head to the PACD and ask a staff for assistance. They will provide you with the PMRF documents as well as assist you with it.

PMRF - (PhilHealth Member Registration Form)



What other Documents should I bring?

Birth Certificate/Baptismal Certificate If Single

• Bring a photo/xerox copy of the member's birth certificate with regisrty number from LCR - (Local Civil Registrar) or PSA - (Philippine Statistics Authority), The member can also bring Baptismal Certificate with registry number.

What about if Married?

Marriage Contract/Certificate of Marriage If Married

• Provide a photo/xerox copy of the member's marriage contract or certificate of marriage.

What if I don't have a birth certificate?

Provide any Government Issued IDs

• Provide a photo/xerox copy of any valid or Government Issued IDs. Driver's License, TIN ID, SSS ID, PNP ID Card, Senior Citizen ID, etc.

How old should I be to become a member?

21 years old above

• The legal age for registring as a member in philhealth is the age of 21 where the member is no longer dependent/benificiaries of their parents.
